About Us!

Uncle Brent and the NoStone


Uncle Brent & the NoStone are bringing Americana-Rock to the stage capable of pumping-out hours of versatile rhythms & all-out good-times. Uncle Brent's imaginative songwriting takes on a wild sound with the addition of the Nostone providing an authentic freedom of expression in the music that can only come from a genuine love of the craft and true desire to entertain. With dynamic sets of songs containing originals and cover-tunes, Uncle Brent and the Nostone are playing gigs on the regular throughout the Austin music-scene & beyond, bringing fun, talented musicianship & songwriting that sincerely connects right back into the spotlight where it belongs.
